About Us

Erbil Information Technology Services: Your Trusted Partner in Technology Founded in 2022, Erbil Information Technology Services has been dedicated to providing tailored technological solutions for companies and end-users. Our goal is to analyze our customers' needs accurately and simplify their business processes with cost-effective and efficient solutions.

To date, we have successfully carried out numerous projects with corporate firms and continue to provide ongoing support through maintenance agreements. Whether you are a corporate or individual client, we are here to make technology easy for you.

Our Mission

At Erbil Information Technology Services, our mission is to simplify and enhance business processes by providing the most accurate and economical technological solutions to our customers. We aim to make technology more accessible, effective, and impactful for everyone, becoming a trusted partner in their digital transformation journey for both individual and corporate users.

Our Vision

As a leading technology company in the digital transformation era, our vision is to deliver innovative and sustainable solutions both locally and globally. We strive to use technology in a human-centric way to shape our customers' futures and consistently add value to their lives.
